About us

We have been operating on the market since 1991 on behalf of Ing.Ján Križan and since 2006 as well as GKK-geodetická služba s.r.o. Our experience in the field of geodesy and cadastre is a guarantee of quality and timely work. In our work we use modern technical and software equipment.

We deal with:

  • Complex geodetic activity
  • Advice on land law and real estate

If you need geodetic work, do not hesitate to contact us and we will be happy to provide you our services.

Services

Realized geodetic and cartographic works are carried out under the guidance of an authorized surveyor and cartographer.

  • property arrangement of land
  • demarcation of land boundaries
  • consultancy in the field of land law and real estate
  • creation of geometric plans according to customer needs
  • surveying and elaboration of address point
  • graphic identification (representation of parcels of legal status - designated operat in valid state of cadastral map)

  • focusing planimetry and altimetry to the required extent
  • preparation of the geodetic part of the documentation of the actual construction of buildings and technological equipment
  • preparation of geodetic part of pre-project documentation
  • control measurement of the terrain in the area of the construction site before the commencement of earthworks
  • post-realization focus of underground and above-ground engineering networks

  • building local stakeout networks
  • creation of planimetry and altimetry for the engineers
  • survey and control of geometric parameters of industrial objects and technological equipment
  • monitoring deformations during construction and after completion
  • demarcation of spatial position of building objects
  • geodetic works on building facades
  • production of cross and longitudinal profiles according to requirements, calculation of cubes
  • surveying and processing of street bands as well as their reconstruction
  • measuring of overhead and underground lines
  • creation and updating of digital technical map of places, races
  • scanning and creation of digital terrain models and 3D models as required
  • comprehensive processing including the creation of an information system and archiving of the geodetic part of the documentation of the actual execution of the construction according to the real works

  • designing land consolidation

Advise

We will gladly advise you in case of doubt. Just contact us by mail or phone.

Do you know where to start?

  • Do you want to settle the land?
    You will need a geometric plan, inheritance, respectively. contracts and documents proving the relationship to the land.
  • Do you want to survey land?
    In this case, it is necessary to define what the subsequent activity is, whether you want to enclose the land, divide the land, sell the land or clearly identify it in the field.
  • Do you want to measure the construction on the plot?
    It is necessary to know whether it is a focus on building approval, or the foundation of an unfinished building in favor of the bank into the land registry.
